About me.

Hi, I’m Atishaya! Feel free to call me Atish though!

You can find my current updated resume here and my contact information to reach out here.

Aside from the professional side of stuff, here are some things about me:

  • Originally from Nepal, I’m currently based in Winnipeg, Canada working towards a Joint Honours Degree in Mathematics and Computer Science with a minor in Statistics.
  • If you couldn’t tell already, I love reading, writing, and researching Mathematics. Some of my favourite fields include Graph Theory, Computational Complexity Theory, and Combinatorics.
  • I currently work as a Frontend Developer at SoAni Tech working towards building and maintaining LG US’ website. I also am currently working as a student researcher for two projects; one in the faculty of Mathematics, the other in the Geometric, Approximation, and Distributed Algorithms (GADA) lab .

  • My Mathematics research currently delves into k-nary trees and HK-property. While the work is very theoretical, I have developed some computer programs to visualize and experiment numerically using Jupyter Notebooks with SageMath.


    My Computer Science research with the GADA lab delves into finding a possible Fixed Parameter Tractable solution or approximation to a specific instance of ad-hoc networks interference in one dimension.


    I aim to go to graduate school and ultimately get a PhD, either in Computer Science or Mathematics.

  • My hobbies include taking long walks, biking, reading, and cooking. I especially like to cook stir fry traditional Nepali dishes and eat them with rice. Rice is the best! Aside from that, I love teaching and tutoring people; especially with software ideas and technologies that I struggled with.
